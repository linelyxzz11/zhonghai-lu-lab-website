The HKUST(GZ) Microelectronics team won first place in the PHM-AP 2025 Data Challenge, held during the Asia Pacific Conference of the Prognostics and Health Management Society in Singapore from December 8 to 11, 2025.

The competition focused on predicting cutter wear in CNC machine tools using time-series data. It is a major biennial competition organized by the international PHM Society and brings together teams from leading universities and companies.

The team, named **Lone warrior**, consisted of Chao Guo, Hongji Pei, Weihang Xu, and Prof. Zhonghai Lu. Their winning algorithm was **Tool Wear Prediction Using Two-Stage Trained Transformer**.

The result demonstrates the team’s research capability in prognostics and health management, intelligent manufacturing, microelectronics, and artificial intelligence.
